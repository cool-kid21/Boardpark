To add a Site to BoardPark.com
To edit your listing

1. Complete all fields in the form below to submit your site to the BoardPark.com database and directory.

2. If approved for inclusion in BoardPark.com, your site will be placed in the appropriate directory and the searchable database.

3. Remember, your site must be directly relevant to BoardPark.com's subject matter. BoardPark.com reserves the right to refuse any submissions it feels do not reflect the subject matter of this Site. BoardPark.com also reserves the right to place any URL in the category it believes to most accurately represent a given site.

If your site is already listed on BoardPark.com and you would like to edit your listing as it appears in the directory and database, or if you would like to add to/change your keywords, send us an e-mail request .

Include your site's name, your URL and the current category (if known). We will forward the information currently in our database and you may e-mail the changes to us.

Please do not use the form below to resubmit a site that is already in the database.
