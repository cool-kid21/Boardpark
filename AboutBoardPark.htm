About BoardPark.com

A comprehensive boardsports portal
BoardPark.com is first and foremost a search and indexing facility dedicated to providing the most comprehensive search capability for boardsports on the World Wide Web.

With the explosion of Web portals recently, BoardPark.com was designed to fill the need for a dedicated search and service site exclusively for the skateboard, snowboard and surfing communities.

BoardPark.com uses a categorical approach to indexing, making it fast and simple to find what you're looking for. BoardPark.com also offers what we believe is the Internet's leading search engine devoted exclusively to skate, snow, surf and other boardsports.

BoardPark's database contains well over 10,000 entries to boardsport sites and continues to grow rapidly.

Built for Speed
BoardPark.com was built for speed—to make it as easy as possible to find information quickly. This is the primary reason for the use of categories in addition to a traditional search engine (although the BoardPark.com search engine is exclusive to boardsports). Other search engines tend to return hundreds and thousands of unrelated sites to an average search query. As a dedicated boardsports portal, BoardPark.com virtually eliminates this wasted time. Visitors spend more time visiting sites they want to visit and less time searching through lines and lines of trash.

For the same reason, BoardPark.com avoids overusing large graphics, Java applets and similar code that can slow the loading time of a given page.

Free Classifieds
In addition to its primary function as a boardsport Web search service, BoardPark.com is committed to providing free services to the boarding community.

Classified ads are provided free of charge to private individuals looking to buy or sell goods on the World Wide Web. To maintain the integrity fo the service, classified ads are not available to corporations or businesses looking to sell their goods. BoardPark offers paid advertising positions at a nominal fee for businesses looking to reach boarders visiting BoardPark.

Corporations can, however, use the service to place Help Wanted ads.

Free e-mail
BoardPark.com offers free e-mail through its special domain: boardparkmail.com.

More to come
BoardPark.com is committed to developing and launching additional free services to online boardsports community.
